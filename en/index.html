What is the EUC TechCon?

The EUC TechCon (previously vEUC TechCon) is an event where the passion for the products and services of Omnissa, and end-user computing (EUC) in general, is shared by the community, to the community.

If it's either sharing best practices, cool solutions, nice projects or other beautiful things around EUC, it's all possible.

You are more than welcome to share your beautiful story during this event!

This event is made possible by the unique collaboration between Previder, ITQ and Omnissa.

When is the EUC TechCon?

The EUC TechCon 2026 takes place on Wednesday, June 17th, 2026.

Where is the EUC TechCon?

The EUC TechCon takes place in Pathé Utrecht Leidsche Rijn.
